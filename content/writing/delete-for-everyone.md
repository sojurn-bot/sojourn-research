**Author's Note:**

*Some messages are deleted because they're too raw, too honest, too scared of rejection. Others are deleted because typing them was enough—the act of articulating the feeling to yourself, even if you can't send it to them yet.*

*But the messages we actually send? Those are acts of courage. Every "I love you" that survives the cursor hovering over delete is a small miracle.*

*This story lives in the gap between what we feel and what we say. In the end, Sam and Jo got there—not by being brave all at once, but by being brave enough, often enough, to let the important things through.*

*The deleted messages are the rehearsals. The sent ones are the performance. Both are part of the love story.*
